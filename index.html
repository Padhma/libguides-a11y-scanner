Troubleshooting

I don't see my bookmarks bar
Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to toggle it on.
Nothing happens when I click the bookmarklet
Make sure you're on a LibGuides page (guides.lib.umich.edu). The scanner only works on actual web pages, not blank tabs or browser settings pages.
How accurate is the scanner?
The scanner uses axe-core (industry standard, used by Microsoft/Google) plus custom LibGuides-specific checks. It catches ~60% of WCAG issues automatically. Some issues require manual review by humans.
